Overseer

Classification: Medium Threat level: Medium

Strengths:

Overseers are the bipedal foot soldiers of the Illuminate collective that watch over and direct the mind-controlled masses of the Voteless. Their vile alien forms are fully-encased in a sturdy but flexible segmented armour, composed of unknown materials likely only found in their own system. As their armour is formed of multiple individual pieces, this renders them more protected than you might think, as if one piece breaks, they still have many others. They also have some degree of martial prowess, as they swing their plasma staves with power and speed, knocking you over should you be unable to avoid. Their weapon of choice also has a ranged functionality, able to fire powerful plasma shots from a distance. Additionally, they carry a small device capable of emitting a strong directional shield that protects the majority of the Overseer when active.

Weaknesses:

The flesh underneath their armour is weak and fragile, and they are susceptible to the stun and poison gas effects. Their shield leaves part of their legs exposed to gunfire.

Recommended strategies:

Utilize stun or gas-based weaponry or stratagems to render them helpless. Medium penetration primaries are recommended. Ensure plasma shot evasion by courageously hiding behind cover.

Alternative strategies:

Focus fire on specific parts of their body to efficiently remove armour and achieve target destruction. Headshots from sufficiently powerful weapons will drop the target instantly. While not ideal, make use of the “dive and dive and dive again” strategy when cover is unavailable. If the target brings up their shield before they can be staggered enough or outright liberated, aim for the legs.
