Harvester

Classification: Heavy Threat level: Medium

Specifications:

Heavily armoured tripedal construct used by the illuminate to invade and conquer, armed with a continuous high-output laser beam that carves through armour and incinerates all it touches, equipped with a close-range arc weapon capable of electrifying anything that gets close. Small shield generator on its back, recharges over time post shield-integrity failure.

Weaknesses:

Its laser beam output “eye” is largely unprotected, legs are less protected in the upper sections.

Recommended strategies:

Employ the use of shield relays to defend against its laser beam, fire anti-tank to medium penetration weaponry at its legs for target destruction. Its shield generator can be destroyed with medium penetration or higher, thus permanently disabling its self-protective capabilities.

Viable strategies:

Fire at its laser beam “eye” with anti-tank weaponry for immediate destruction, this is however a much harder target to hit than the legs.
