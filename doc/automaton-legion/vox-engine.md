# Vox Engine

**Classification:** Super Heavy
**Threat level:** Extreme

---

## Specifications:

Vox Engines are gargantuan siege machines designed for all out war, each one piloted by a Cyborg traitor and almost entirely covered in advanced thick armour-plating. Serving as the base of this abomination are four treads connected to a chassis, upon which sits an omnidirectional turret that can aim anywhere its system deems optimal for oppressing Democracy. Atop the treads are two large laser gatling guns each equipped with a pale yellow laser sight. The omnidirectional turret has two humungous burst-fire laser cannons built onto the sides of the turret. Not only that, it has two large guided missile pods attached to the back of the turret, making them roughly as dangerous as an automaton outpost, except mobile. They are also equipped with tyrannical propagandic speakers that spread their treasonous ways to all within earshot.

## Weaknesses:

As with all other heavy units utilized by the steel legion, Vox Engines have heat vents to expel excess heat. There are two vents at the back of their base and one underneath, that when destroyed, reveal a critical flaw in their design - a small hatch, that when a grenade is thrown into, results in catastrophic destruction. Its' omnidirectional turret also features these weaknesses, with three longer but thinner heat vents in the back. The vents on the base and underneath are considerably fragile, able to be destroyed by sidearms. The vents on the turret however are more durable, but vulnerable all the same. The laser gatlings can be destroyed with medium pen, or a brave Helldiver with a machete. Their missile pods can also be destroyed with medium pen, and their cannons require anti-tank to damage.

## Recommended strategies:

Due to the extreme threat they pose, units in the field should exercise extreme caution when engaging, if at all. When engaged, try to remove as much of its offensive capabilities as possible, so as to allow for a less risky approach behind it for heat vent destruction. Disengage from the target if possible, upon determination of sub-optimal target destruction to mission viability ratio. Enough firepower directed at the turret's heat vents will eventually destroy the target.

## Alternative strategies:

It has a command module that houses the pilot at the topmost middle of the front of the turret, which requires anti-tank to damage. Direct all anti-tank at this target until it goes up in flames. Its tracks can be damaged and destroyed with heavy penetration weaponry.
