# Berserker

**Classification:** Medium
**Threat level:** Medium to High

---

## Specifications:
Berserkers are the lightly-armoured chainsaw maniacs of the legion, hell-bent on rushing down any liberated individual heedless of their own continued functionality. They use their lack of armour to their advantantage, charging chainsaws up with ferocious speed. Each of their two arm-saws are built for maximum pain and suffering, ensuring that whomever of our brave freedom fighters survives the attack, is left with an injury.
